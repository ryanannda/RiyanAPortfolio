Data Excel - Sales Report With Pivot Table

Presenting sales report data from 2023-2024 based on various categories such as country, customer segment, and product type. This data includes information on selling price, production price, discounts, and profits obtained from each transaction using formulas.

Notes

You can view or download the entire excel file here: Data Sales Report.xlsx

Explanation of Calculation for Each Column Category

  • Total Sales Before Discount
    Total Sales Before Discount = Total Units Sold x Selling Price/Unit

    Formula =[@[Total Unit Terjual]]*[@[Harga Jual/Unit]]

  • Discount Percentage
    If the discount type is High then the discount is 20%, if Medium then 10%, if Low then 5%, if No Discount then 0%

    Formula =IF(I2="Tinggi"; 20%; IF(I2="Sedang"; 10%; IF(I2="Rendah"; 5%; 0)))

  • Total Discount
    Total Discount = Total Sales Before Discount x Discount Percentage

    Formula =[@[Total Penjualan Sebelum Diskon]]*[@[Persentase Diskon]]

  • Total Sales After Discount
    Total Penjualan Setelah Diskon = Total Penjualan Sebelum Diskon - Total Diskon

    Formula =[@[Total Penjualan Sebelum Diskon]]-[@[Total Diskon]]

  • Production Capital
    Modal Produksi = Total Unit Terjual x Harga Produksi/Unit

    Formula =[@[Total Unit Terjual]]*[@[Harga Produksi/Unit]]

  • Profit
    Profit = Total Sales After Discount - Production Capital

    Formula =[@[Total Penjualan Setelah Diskon]]-[@[Modal Produksi]]
